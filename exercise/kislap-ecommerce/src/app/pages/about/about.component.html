About Kislap

Celebrating Filipino Beauty Through Hybrid Makeup & Skincare

Our Story

Kislap was born from a simple yet powerful belief: Filipino beauty deserves products that are specifically designed for our unique skin types, climate, and cultural preferences. Founded in 2020 by Maria Santos, a beauty enthusiast who struggled to find makeup that worked perfectly with her Filipino skin.

After years of mixing different products to achieve the perfect look, Maria realized there was a gap in the market. Why should Filipinas have to choose between makeup and skincare? Why not have both in one revolutionary product?

Today, Kislap is proud to be the first Filipino brand to specialize in hybrid makeup-skincare products, helping thousands of women achieve their perfect glow while caring for their skin.

Our Mission

To empower Filipino women by providing high-quality hybrid makeup and skincare products that celebrate our natural beauty while addressing our unique skin needs. We believe that beauty should enhance, not hide, the beautiful features that make us uniquely Filipino.
